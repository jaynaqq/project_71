Whats New

  • New School Geography Lab

    Geography LabTo provide the best in class education to our students, we have state of the art, well equipped Geography Lab, wherein, our aspiring students can learn the basics of the subject by imbibing the practical know-how of the subject. The main tools and techniques that we use for making our students practically aware of the theories taught in the classroom are Smart Board, Working Models, Illuminated Globe etc.Smart Board is the latest tool that helps in the explanation of chapter through PPTs, videos, data analysis and map making.In future, we plan to enhance our capabilities by adding Remote Sensing and GIS which shall help students to learn and decide for the future specific selection of courses for their growth and development in higher studies.

  • New school Library

    Good books help in developing overall personality of itslibrary readers. Therefore, D.A.V. has laid stress on providing the students a library which is propitious, useful and complete. The school has a library for each - the junior segment, the secondary and the senior secondary segment. The libraries are computerized, making the working effective and easy for the staff and the students. The school use this library extensively for all co-curricular & extra curricular activities like debates, quiz, talk-shows.
